Travel Planning

This app helps you plan your trip if you want to go somewhere. It provides information on the following:
Famous Food: What are the famous foods at your destination?
Places to Visit: What are the popular places you can visit there?
Accommodation: Where can you stay during your trip?
Transportation: Which vehicle should you use to travel?
Group Details: How many people are traveling with you?
Based on this information, the app calculates how much
budget you will need for your trip. Go
